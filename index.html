LaMott Ebron

LaMott L. Ebron is a lifelong resident of Philadelphia, PA. After completing his grade school education at Simon Gratz Highschool in 2005 he began a career in Motorsports. His career in motorsports landed him a job with Hendrick Motorsports in Concord N.C. The premier team in NASCAR. His stay, however brief, served to LaMott a new perspective on life and it was in 2008 he returned to Philadelphia to begin his life in public service. He chose to run for city committee as and served one term as Ward leader for the city's 13th ward. This lead to an unsuccessful bid for State-Representative in 2012, a lose that would change his attitude towards politics forever, giving new life to LaMott's entrepreneurial aspirations. After a 2 year apprenticeship with K.B.E Contractors LaMott was ready to plant his feet and start his own construction business "Ebron Built" in 2016. After completing dozens of home rehabs and growing his business to employ over 10 employees by the end of the 2018 calander year, he learned his longtime girlfriend was expecting their first child. Public servant, builder, and now father LaMott Ebron is now ready to take on computer programming as he is currrently enrolled in the the University of Pennsylvainia's Coding Bootcamp.
